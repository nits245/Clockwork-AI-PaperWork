## TRANSITION TYPES

### 1. Room Change Transitions (REF-005)

**Shared Room → Personal Room:**
- Availability confirmation required
- Rate adjustment: From ${finance.accommodation.weekly_rent} shared to $[PERSONAL_RATE] personal
- Personal room exclusive nighttime occupancy rights
- Loss of coordinator access (standard tenant rights apply)

**Personal Room → Shared Room:**
- Compatibility assessment with current room occupants
- Rate reduction: From $[PERSONAL_RATE] to ${finance.accommodation.weekly_rent} shared
- Coordinator access rights if participants present in room
- Flexible bed configuration rules apply

### 2. Status Change Transitions (REF-005)

**Tenant → Team Member:**
- Skills assessment and demonstration of program commitment
- Potential accommodation cost reduction through profit-sharing
- Additional program responsibilities and time commitments
- Access to co-op financial benefits and employment pathways

**Team Member → Tenant:**
- 4-week notice period to transfer responsibilities to replacement
- Loss of profit-sharing and cost reduction benefits
- Return to standard tenant-only accommodation rates
- Retention of developed skills and program connections

**Trial Participant → Tenant:**
- Successful completion of assessment period
- Transition from guest status to formal tenancy rights
- Payment of accommodation bond as per Residential Tenancies Act
- Full tenant protections and quiet enjoyment rights

### 3. Model Conversion Transitions (REF-022)

**Flexible Tenancy → Standard Group Lease:**
- Unanimous consent required from all current tenants
- Termination of individual rolling lease agreements
- Execution of new 6-month group lease with fixed capacity pricing
- Total cost: 6 beds × ${finance.accommodation.weekly_rent} = $[TOTAL] weekly
- Group assumes collective responsibility and decision-making authority

**Standard Group Lease → Flexible Tenancy (Reversion):**
- Triggered by failure to maintain minimum 4 people for 60+ days
- Automatic reversion without group consent required
- Individual tenancy agreements for remaining residents
- Return to pay-per-occupied-bed pricing model

## NOTICE PERIODS AND TIMELINES

### Standard Notice Requirements
**Room Changes:** 2 weeks advance notice for availability planning  
**Status Changes:** 4 weeks advance notice for responsibility transfer  
**Model Conversion:** Immediate upon unanimous group agreement  
**Emergency Transitions:** 48 hours for safety-related moves

### Transition Process
1. **Application Submission:** Written request with reasons and preferred timeline
2. **Assessment Period:** 7-14 days for evaluation and approval process
3. **Agreement Execution:** Signing of transition agreement and any lease variations
4. **Implementation:** Physical move and status change effective date
5. **Follow-up Review:** 30-day check-in to assess transition success

---

## REVERSION CLAUSES

### Unsuccessful Transitions
**Trial Period:** 30-day trial period for significant status changes  
**Performance Issues:** Reversion to previous arrangement if standards not met  
**Household Conflicts:** Return to previous setup if compatibility problems arise  
**Financial Difficulties:** Options for modified arrangements or return to previous status

### Protection Measures
**No Penalty Reversion:** Return to previous arrangement without financial penalty during trial period  
**Support Services:** Additional support and coaching during transition period  
**Alternative Options:** Exploration of different arrangements if initial transition unsuccessful
