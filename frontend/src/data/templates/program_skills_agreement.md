## PROGRAM OVERVIEW AND MISSION

### Program Mission (REF-002)
{entity.trading.name} operates as a sustainability and lifestyle program focused on:
- Supporting individuals to develop sustainable living practices
- Building skills in domestic management, food safety, and ecological responsibility
- Creating supportive environments that prioritize environmental outcomes
- Facilitating transition from unsustainable lifestyle patterns to regenerative practices
- Supporting local flora, fauna, and ecological systems

### Program Objectives
This agreement establishes framework for skills development and training in:
- **Domestic Management:** Cleaning, organization, household systems
- **Food Safety and Commercial Kitchen Operations:** HACCP, temperature control, sanitization
- **Sustainable Living Practices:** Environmental stewardship, resource conservation
- **Basic Handyman and Maintenance Skills:** Property care, equipment maintenance
- **Customer Service and Retail Operations:** Communication, sales, inventory management
- **Community Engagement:** Collaborative living, conflict resolution, teamwork
