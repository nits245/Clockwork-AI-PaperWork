## MISSION PROTECTION AND ANTI-ELITE CAPTURE (REF-024)

### Minimum Occupancy Requirements
**Minimum Tenants:** 4 people must be maintained within 60 days of any departure  
**Recruitment Responsibility:** Group responsible for advertising and filling vacant beds  
**Management Override:** If group fails to maintain minimum, management may advertise vacant beds  
**Reversion Clause:** If occupancy falls below minimum for 60 days, lease reverts to Flexible Tenancy Model

### New Member Selection Process
**Group Decision Making:** New members require majority consent from existing tenants  
**Valid Refusal Reasons Only:** Group may only refuse candidates for legitimate safety or compatibility concerns  
**Management Review:** Disputed refusals subject to management review and override

**Valid Reasons for Refusal:**
- Criminal history involving violence, theft, or property damage
- Substance abuse issues posing household safety risks
- History of harassment, intimidation, or threatening behavior
- Fundamental conflict with sustainability mission and program values
- Serious incompatibility with existing household members (documented)
- Inability to meet basic safety and hygiene standards

**Invalid Reasons for Refusal:**
- Race, religion, nationality, sexual orientation, gender identity
- Disability (unless poses genuine safety risk that cannot be reasonably accommodated)
- Social class, education level, or employment status
- Personal preferences without documented compatibility issues
- Financial status (excluding inability to meet rent obligations)

### Financial Accessibility Protection
**Mission Alignment:** Group arrangements must remain compatible with program sustainability mission  
**No Exclusivity:** Cannot exclude candidates based on desire for program participation  
**Accessibility Maintenance:** Lease must serve community objectives rather than exclusive arrangements

All tenants must comply with:
1. **Safety Priority:** No endangering household or premises safety
2. **Peaceful Environment:** No significant disruption to household harmony

4. **Child-Friendly Environment:** Appropriate language and behavior (subject to adult kitchen option)
5. **Smoke/Vape Free:** Completely smoke and vape free premises ($150 violation fee)
6. **Drug Free:** No illicit drugs or addiction-related substances
