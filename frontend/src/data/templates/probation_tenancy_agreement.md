## PROBATION TENANCY FRAMEWORK

### Legal Status and Tenant Rights
**Full Tenancy Rights:** Complete protection under Victorian Residential Tenancies Act 1997  
**VCAT Jurisdiction:** Standard tenancy dispute resolution available  
**Quiet Enjoyment:** Full tenant rights to reasonable privacy and property use  
**No Eviction for Program Performance:** Tenancy cannot be terminated for failing to advance to team member status

### Program Evaluation Component
**Skills Development Assessment:** Weekly evaluation of domestic, kitchen, and community participation competencies  
**Compatibility Review:** Ongoing assessment of household integration and collaborative living abilities  
**Sustainability Alignment:** Evaluation of commitment to environmental practices and program mission  
**Leadership Potential:** Assessment of mentoring capability and community contribution readiness

### Probation Period Structure
**Minimum Duration:** 8 weeks required before team member advancement consideration  
**Extension Option:** Up to 12 weeks maximum if additional skill development needed  
**Assessment Criteria:** Competency demonstration across all domestic and program requirements per REF-026  
**Advancement Decision:** Management discretion for team member role - advancement optional, not automatic

---

## SKILLS PROGRESSION REQUIREMENTS

### Week 1-2: Foundation Establishment
**Emergency and Safety Mastery:** Complete understanding of safety protocols and emergency procedures  
**Basic Domestic Competency:** Daily cleaning responsibilities, room maintenance, shared area care  
**Kitchen Safety Certification:** Food handling, sanitation, and commercial safety standards  
**Household Integration:** Positive interaction with existing household members and conflict resolution

### Week 3-4: Advanced Domestic Systems
**Extended Property Management:** Bathroom, outdoor area, and specialized cleaning responsibilities  
**Commercial Kitchen Operations:** Food preparation, inventory management, and customer service basics  
**Team Coordination:** Roster participation, quality assurance, and new member orientation assistance  
**Environmental Stewardship:** Sustainable practices, waste management, and ecological responsibility

### Week 5-8: Leadership Development Assessment
**Mentoring Capability:** Demonstrated ability to teach and support newer participants  
**Conflict Resolution:** Effective communication and problem-solving in household situations  
**Program Mission Commitment:** Active contribution to sustainability goals and community building  
**Reliability and Consistency:** Sustained performance across all program requirements

### Extension Criteria (Week 9-12 if needed)
**Skills Gap Identification:** Specific areas requiring additional development time  
**Personal Circumstances:** Accommodation for individual learning pace or external challenges  
**Household Dynamics:** Resolution of compatibility issues through additional support and mediation  
**Professional Development:** Enhanced training for leadership roles requiring extended preparation

## HOUSE RULES AND STANDARDS

### Core House Rules (REF-011)
**Universal Application:** All probation tenancy participants must comply with foundational house rules  
**Environmental Standards:** Quiet hours, smoke/vape free premises, drug-free environment  
**Community Standards:** Child-friendly environment, respectful communication, property care  
**Safety Priority:** No endangering household or premises safety at any time

### Adult Kitchen Environment Option (REF-004.1)
**Group Consent Required:** Unanimous household agreement for "Ramsay Kitchen" profanity allowance  
**Child Safety Override:** Automatically disabled when individuals under 16 present  
**Broadcasting Considerations:** Special consent required if educational broadcasting active during sessions  
**Revocation Rights:** Any household member may revoke consent with 48-hour notice

### Program Integration Standards
**Skills Development Commitment:** Active participation in training and assessment activities  
**Mentoring Receptivity:** Openness to instruction and feedback from coordinators and experienced members  
**Community Contribution:** Positive participation in household activities and collaborative decision-making  
**Sustainability Practices:** Demonstrated commitment to environmental responsibility and regenerative living

## COORDINATOR ACCESS AND PROGRAM DELIVERY

### Mixed Occupancy Room Coordination (REF-007)
**Access Rights Application:** Coordinator access activated only when trial participants or team members present in shared rooms  
**Probation Tenant Rights:** Standard tenant privacy rights when occupying rooms with other tenants only  
**Program Activity Access:** Entry permitted for skills training, assessment, and mentoring during business hours  
**Personal Area Protection:** 72-hour notice required for changes to bed areas and personal storage

### Program Delivery Integration
**Skills Training Sessions:** Coordinated access for domestic, kitchen, and community skills instruction  
**Assessment Activities:** Periodic evaluation sessions and competency demonstrations  
**Mentoring Opportunities:** Participation in orientation and support for newer trial participants  
**Leadership Development:** Progressive responsibility increase based on demonstrated capability

## TRANSITION PATHWAYS

### Team Member Advancement Option
**Eligibility Review:** Comprehensive assessment after minimum 8-week probation completion  
**Skills Demonstration:** Competency across all domestic, kitchen, and community requirements  
**Household Endorsement:** Positive evaluation from existing household members and coordinators  
**Financial Benefits:** Profit-sharing eligibility and accommodation cost reduction opportunities upon advancement

### Continued Tenancy Option
**Standard Residential Arrangement:** Continuation as regular tenant without team member responsibilities  
**No Advancement Penalty:** Tenancy cannot be terminated for choosing not to advance to team member status  
**Skills Retention:** Continued access to training and development opportunities on voluntary basis  
**Community Participation:** Ongoing involvement in household activities and collaborative living

### Extension and Support Options
**Probation Extension:** Up to 12 weeks maximum if additional skill development time needed  
**Professional Support:** Trauma-informed care and mental health support coordination where appropriate  
**Flexibility Accommodation:** Modified expectations for documented challenges or special circumstances  
**Crisis Intervention:** Enhanced support services and intervention protocols for at-risk situations

---

## TERMINATION AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

### Standard Tenancy Termination Procedures
**Tenancy Law Compliance:** All termination procedures per Victorian Residential Tenancies Act 1997  
**Notice Periods:** Standard notice requirements for end of term or breach-based termination  
**VCAT Jurisdiction:** Victorian Civil and Administrative Tribunal available for dispute resolution  
**Program Performance Separation:** Tenancy termination separate from program advancement decisions

### Enhanced Support Before Termination
**Trauma-Informed Approach:** Additional support and intervention before penalties for documented trauma survivors  
**Professional Assessment:** Differentiation between trauma responses and willful misconduct  
**Graduated Intervention:** Counseling and support services offered before financial penalties or termination procedures  
**Crisis Prevention:** Proactive intervention and de-escalation for identified risk situations
