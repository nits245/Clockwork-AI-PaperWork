## MISSION ROOM CONCEPT

### Integrated Program Accommodation
This accommodation option provides integrated program learning spaces where residents participate in an active educational environment. Mission rooms combine residential accommodation with hands-on learning opportunities, skills development, and community engagement activities.

**Key Features:**
- Shared learning environment with program participants
- Active skills development and training opportunities
- Community engagement and collaborative projects
- Flexible space usage for educational activities
- Integration with sustainability and lifestyle programs

### Room Function and Usage
Mission rooms serve dual purposes:
- **Residential:** Accommodation for program-aligned residents
- **Educational:** Active learning spaces for program delivery
- **Community:** Collaborative areas for group activities and training
