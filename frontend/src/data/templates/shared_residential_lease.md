## SHARED RESIDENTIAL ACCOMMODATION TERMS

### Nature of Shared Accommodation
This is shared residential accommodation where:
- **Landlord and family members reside on-premises** as part of the shared living arrangement
- Common areas are accessible to all residents including landlord and family
- Individual rooms may be shared or private as specified
- All residents must comply with house rules for community harmony
- Business operations may occur during designated hours
- Landlord's residence on-premises is integral to the accommodation model and program operations

### Excluded Area Protocols
**Shareholder/Director Room Usage:**
- Usage governed by company board decisions and director discretion
- May function as workspace, meeting room, accommodation, or other company purposes
- Changes to usage arrangements may occur without notice to residential tenants
- All users must comply with noise restrictions during quiet hours: {hours.quiet.winter.start}-{hours.quiet.winter.end} (Winter/Autumn), {hours.quiet.summer.start}-{hours.quiet.summer.end} (Spring/Summer)
- Emergency access and safety protocols apply to all premises users
- No tenant rights or claims extend to excluded area

**Tenant Acknowledgment:**
Tenant acknowledges and agrees that one room remains under company control for shareholder/director use and is not part of this residential tenancy. Tenant has no rights, claims, or expectations regarding this excluded area or its usage.

### Quiet Enjoyment Within Shared Context
Renter is entitled to quiet enjoyment balanced with:
- Shared nature of accommodation
- Business operations during designated hours
- Community living requirements
- House rules for harmonious living
